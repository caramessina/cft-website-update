Rhetorical Genre Studies defines genre as a social actions that are recurring and re-acted in particular communities; engaging in a genre means taking into account the conventions of that genre, the ideologies embedded within those conventions, who has say over these conventions, and how engaging in these genres help community formation. These codes include angst, fluff, fix-it fics, and more.
