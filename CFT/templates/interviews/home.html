Interviews

Six fanfic writers—three who wrote The Legend of Korra and three who wrote Game of Thrones fanfics—share their experiences writing these fics, their perspectives on the fan community, and the ways in which they think about race, gender, sexuality, and/or culture in their fan engagement. The transcribed interviews and demographic information have been published with each writers’ explicit consent (IRB number NUM).
